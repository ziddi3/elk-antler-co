Our Story

From Wild Sheds to Happy Dogs

Elk & Antler Co. began with a simple love for dogs and the great outdoors. Every antler we sell is naturally shed — elk drop their antlers each year, so no animal is ever harmed in the gathering.

We hand-gather Grade A antlers, remove any sharp edges, wash them, and meticulously inspect each one before it reaches your pup. The result is a clean, safe, long-lasting chew that's as wholesome as a treat can be.

We also offer organic wheat berries — a wholesome whole grain you can sprout into nutritious pet grass or cook as a fiber-rich food topper. Real ingredients, real benefits, made with love.

Feeding & Safety Guide

Chew Happy, Chew Safe

A few simple tips go a long way to keeping your best friend safe and happy.

📏

Pick the Right Size

Choose a chew in proportion to your dog's weight. They should be able to hold it in their paws and chew one end.

👀

Always Supervise

Keep an eye on your dog while chewing, especially aggressive chewers, and store chews safely when unattended.

🔄

Inspect & Replace

Regularly check for wear and discard a chew before it becomes small enough to be a choking hazard.

💧

Fresh Water

Always keep fresh drinking water available to support healthy digestion during chew time.
